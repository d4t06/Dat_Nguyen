## Motherboard

### Board size (Form)

<CustomImage src="/images/notes/pc-things/board_size.png" width={700} height={400}/>

- ATX (Advanced Technology eXtended): To nhất (30.5 x 24.4 cm)

- Micro-ATX (mATX): 24.4 x 24.4 cm, ít khi PCIe hơn ATC

- Mini-ITX (mITX):  17 x 17 cm nhỏ nhất, for compact or small form factor (SFF)

### Commom socket

### Intel

- Socket intel thường hay thay đổi

- LGA 1700: Used for Intel 12th, 13th, and 14th Gen Core processors (e.g., Alder Lake, Raptor Lake, Raptor Lake Refresh).

- LGA 1851: Used for newer Intel processors like Arrow Lake.

### AMD

- Socket amd ít thay đổi hơn, hổ trợ nâng cấp lâu dài

- AM4: Supported multiple generations of AMD Ryzen CPUs (e.g., Ryzen 1000 to Ryzen 5000 series).

- AM5: The current socket for AMD Ryzen 7000 series and newer CPUs

## CPU

### Hậu tố (Postfix)

- K: CPU can be easily overclocked (e.g., i7-14700K).

- F: No integrated graphics (requires a dedicated graphics card) (e.g., i3-12100F).

- T: lower TDP (Thermal Design Power), designed for energy efficiency and compact systems (e.g., i7-14700T).

- U: Ultra-low power, designed for thin and light laptops, emphasizing battery life (e.g., i7-1355U).

- H: High-performance graphics, typically found in laptops with robust integrated graphics (e.g., i7-13700H).

- KF: Unlocked and no integrated graphics.

- S: Special Edition, often signifying higher clock speeds or unique features compared to standard models.

### Tốc độ theo giao thức

|  | Read | Write | Socket |
| :------------------------ | :-------------------------------------- | :---------------------- | :---------------------------- |
| **SATA III** | Up to 550 MB/s | Up to 500 MB/s | 2.5-inch, M2 Sata (B+M key), mSATA |
| **PCIe 3.0 x4** | 2,500 - 3,500 MB/s | 1,500 - 3,000 MB/s | M2 (M key) |
| **PCIe 4.0 x4** | 5,000 - 7,000+ MB/s | 4,000 - 7,000+ MB/s | M2 (M key) |
| **PCIe 5.0 x4** | 10,000 - 14,000+ MB/s | 10,000 - 14,000+ MB/s | M2 (M key) |


## PCIe speed per lane

- PCIe 1.0 (2003): 250 MB/s.
- PCIe 2.0 (2007): 500 MB/s.
- PCIe 3.0 (2010): ~985 MB/s (approximately 1 GB/s).
- PCIe 4.0 (2017): ~1.97 GB/s.
- PCIe 5.0 (2019): ~3.94 GB/s.

### M2

#### B, M Keys

<CustomImage src="/images/notes/pc-things/keys-b-m.jpg" width={700} height={400}/>

- M Key (PCIe x4): NVMe SSDs

- B Key (PCIe x2): Used for SATA, SSDs WWAN (Wireless Wide Area Network)

- B + M Key (PCIe x2 - Module only): Thiết kế để cắm module vào B key hoặc M key điều được

<CustomImage src="/images/notes/pc-things/b+m_key_ssd.jpg" width={700}/>

- Trên thị truờng cũng có Ssd NVME dùng B+M key nhưng ít, tốc độ sẽ chậm hơn do B key chỉ có 2 lanes PCIe nhưng bù lại là tăng khả năng tăng thích nhiều slot m2 hơn

#### A, E Keys

<CustomImage src="/images/notes/pc-things/keys-a-e.jpg" width={700}/>

- A Key (PCIe x2): Used for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, WWAN, GPS, and NFC cards.

- E Key (PCIe x2): Often used for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth

- A + E Key (Module only): Thiết kế để cắm module vào socket A key hoặc E key điều được, thường là các card Wi-Fi tích hợp Bluetooth.
